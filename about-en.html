Hello, I'm Ursula.
I am a dancer, choreographer and dance educator.

As a contemporary dancer and choreographer, I create dance performances and perform on stage or in other settings - both in my own works and for other choreographers.

I also offer workshops on dance improvisation. My workshops are aimed at people who like to play with movement and want to learn or develop dance-improv skills.

About Ursula


Ursula studied contemporary dance at the Anton Bruckner University Linz (AT) and at La Manufacture, Lausanne (CH) with a focus on creation and dance pedagogy.

She has worked with artists such as Thomas Hauert, Fabrice Mazliah, Jeremy Nelson, Martin Kilvady, David Zambrano, Mal Pelo, Àngels Margarit and Michael Turinsky, among others, and has performed throughout Switzerland at the STEPS Festival, Paris (Centre National de la Danse - CND), Graz (Mumuth, Schauspielhaus, La Strada), Linz (Kunstuni, Raumschiff, KAPU), Vienna (raw matters, Brick-15) and Girona (Centre Cívic Ter).

In 2019 she received the starting grant for music and performing arts from the Federal Ministry for the EU, Arts and Culture. She lives and works in Graz and Girona (Catalonia).

In her dance performances she deals with subjects that are close to her heart; such as feminism, languages ​​and identity. From a sociological point of view, she creates dance performances with humour, sensuality and a strong physicality.

Her dance performances have been invited to the festivals spleen*trieb (Graz 2020), newsOFFstyria (Graz 2021) and the Center Cívic Ter (Girona 2021). In 2022 she has been invited to the Viertelfestival NÖ
